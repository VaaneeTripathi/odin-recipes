Pesto Sauce

Description

This pesto sauce recipe is a quick and easy pasta topping. A great change from red sauce.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups fresh basil leaves
  • 1 ½ cups chopped walnuts
  • 4 cloves garlic, peeled
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Blend together basil leaves, nuts, garlic, and cheese in a food processor until finely chopped.
  3. Pour in oil slowly with the processor running. Season with salt and pepper.
